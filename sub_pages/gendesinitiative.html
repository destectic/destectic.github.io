Consortium Initiative

Reimagining Generative Design Across Disciplines in the Era of AI

This consortium brings together an international and transdisciplinary network of scholars and practitioners to reframe generative design (GD) as a knowledge system in the age of AI. While grounded in design, this initiative includes generative systems across biology, artificial intelligence, education, and engineering—recognizing shared logics and methodological synergies.

Supported by the University of Calgary's Transdisciplinary Connector Grant, the initiative consists of a multi-stage process culminating in a public exhibition and a scholarly publication.

Key Milestones

  • Nov 2025 – International Symposium (Hybrid, Calgary)
  • Dec 2025 – Shared Conceptual Framework (Taxonomy + Diagram)
  • July 2026 – Spin-off Exhibition (University of Calgary / University of Arkansas)
  • May 2027 – Edited Volume Publication
  • July 2027 – Flagship International Exhibition

Expected Impacts

  • Shape the intellectual foundation of generative design in the AI era
  • Join a globally-connected, multi-institutional collaboration
  • Co-author and co-curate the consortium's key outcomes
  • Advance research, pedagogy, and public discourse around GD
